Axis Bank Business Loans

Axis Bank Business Loans are designed to empower entrepreneurs and business owners with hassle-free access to finance. Whether you're expanding operations, upgrading infrastructure, or managing cash flow, these loans offer tailored financial solutions to meet your growing business needs.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Collateral-free loans up to ₹50 lakhs for eligible businesses.
  • Quick approval and fast disbursal with minimal documentation.
  • Flexible repayment tenure ranging from 12 to 60 months.
  • Competitive interest rates based on business profile and creditworthiness.
  • Pre-approved offers for existing Axis Bank customers.
  • Online application and real-time tracking available.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant should be a self-employed individual, sole proprietor, partnership firm, or private limited company.
  • Minimum business vintage of 3 years with consistent income flow.
  • Good credit score (typically 700+ preferred).
  • Applicant should be between 21 and 65 years of age.
  • Business should be registered and operational in India.

Loan Offers

  • Unsecured Business Loans: No collateral required for loans up to ₹50 lakhs.
  • Working Capital Loans: Meet day-to-day operational expenses with flexible overdraft limits.
  • Term Loans: Fixed repayment structure for business expansion or asset acquisition.
  • Equipment Finance: Finance for purchasing machinery, tools, or technology upgrades.
  • Loan for Women Entrepreneurs: Special schemes with concessions in interest rate and documentation.

Fees & Charges

  • Processing Fee: Up to 2% of the loan amount + GST.
  • Prepayment Charges: Up to 4% depending on loan type and tenure.
  • Interest Rate: Starting from 14% per annum (subject to eligibility).
  • Late Payment Charges: 2% per month on the overdue amount.

Working Capital Benefits

Axis Bank offers overdraft and cash credit facilities to help businesses meet short-term cash needs. These facilities come with flexible usage and interest charged only on the amount utilized, offering better liquidity management.

Equipment & Asset Loans

  • Loan facility for purchasing equipment, plant, or machinery.
  • Attractive terms with structured repayment options.
  • Helps in upgrading technology and improving productivity.

Digital Loan Application

Apply for your business loan online through the Axis Bank website or mobile app. Track application status in real time and receive approval in just a few working days.

Collateral-Free Loans

  • Loans up to ₹50 lakhs with no security or collateral needed.
  • Ideal for service providers, retailers, wholesalers, and manufacturers.
  • Based on business performance, turnover, and repayment history.

How to Apply

  • Visit the Axis Bank official website or nearest branch.
  • Choose "Business Loan" under the loan products section.
  • Fill out the online application form with business and financial details.
  • Upload necessary documents or visit a branch for verification.
  • Receive approval and disbursal post verification.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card and Aadhaar Card of proprietor/partners/directors.
  • Business Registration Certificate/GST Registration.
  • Bank statements for the last 6 months.
  • Income Tax Returns and financials for the last 2 years.
  • Address proof of business premises.

Conclusion

Axis Bank Business Loans are a dependable financial tool for small and medium enterprises seeking growth and stability. With quick processing, flexible repayment, and minimal paperwork, these loans help business owners meet their goals with ease and efficiency.
