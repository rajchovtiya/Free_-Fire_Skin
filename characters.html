Axis Bank Magnus Credit Card

Overview

The Axis Bank Magnus Credit Card is a premium lifestyle card tailored for high-spending individuals who want the best in luxury, travel, and rewards. With exclusive benefits such as complimentary domestic and international lounge access, hotel privileges, and concierge services, the Magnus card stands as a top-tier offering in the Axis Bank credit card portfolio.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Complimentary domestic and international airport lounge access.
  • Buy One Get One Free movie offer on BookMyShow (up to ₹500 per ticket).
  • Complimentary Club Marriott membership for dining and stay benefits.
  • Unlimited concierge assistance for travel, dining, and lifestyle services.
  • Exclusive travel benefits with premium hotel chains and airlines.
  • Earn 12 Edge Reward Points per ₹200 spent (except for fuel and specific categories).

Eligibility Criteria

  • Primary cardholder should be aged between 21 to 70 years.
  • Minimum annual income of ₹18 lakhs.
  • Salaried or self-employed professionals with stable income history.
  • Strong CIBIL score (preferably 750 and above).

Fees & Charges

  • Annual Fee: ₹10,000 + GST (waived off on annual spending above ₹15 lakhs).
  • Joining Fee: ₹10,000 + GST (includes welcome benefits worth ₹10,000).
  • Interest Rate: 2.5% per month (30% per annum).
  • Foreign Currency Mark-up Fee: 2% of the transaction value.
  • Cash Withdrawal Fee: 2.5% or ₹500, whichever is higher.

Reward Program

  • 12 Edge Rewards per ₹200 on all spends (excluding fuel and EMIs).
  • Bonus reward points on annual spending milestones.
  • Rewards can be redeemed for flights, hotels, products, gift vouchers, and more.

Travel Benefits

  • Unlimited access to international lounges via Priority Pass membership.
  • Complimentary domestic lounge access at major Indian airports.
  • Concierge travel bookings, including hotels, flights, and holidays.
  • Discounted fares and upgrades with partnered airlines.

Lifestyle & Dining

  • Club Marriott membership offering discounts up to 20% on dining and stays.
  • 24x7 concierge service for reservations, delivery, gift recommendations, and travel assistance.
  • Dining offers at 5-star hotel restaurants across India.

Insurance & Protection

  • Air accident cover up to ₹2.5 crore.
  • Lost card liability protection if reported within 48 hours.
  • Medical emergency cover while traveling internationally.

How to Apply?

  • Visit the official Axis Bank website or nearest branch.
  • Fill out the application form with valid income and KYC documents.
  • Receive a call from the Axis Bank credit card team for verification.
  • Get your card delivered within 7-10 working days post-approval.

Why Choose Magnus Credit Card?

The Magnus card is an elite offering suitable for those who travel frequently and enjoy luxury. With unmatched privileges, curated travel perks, and an exclusive concierge desk, this card is ideal for high-net-worth individuals seeking seamless financial experiences and value.

Conclusion

The Axis Bank Magnus Credit Card is crafted for those who value luxury, exclusivity, and personalized service. Though it comes with a high annual fee, the unmatched rewards, travel benefits, and lifestyle privileges make it well worth it for individuals who want premium banking experiences.
