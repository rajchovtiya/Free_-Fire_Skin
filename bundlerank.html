Rewards Credit Card – Overview

Rewards credit cards are a popular type of credit card that offer points, miles, or cashback every time you make a purchase. These cards are designed to benefit individuals who use their credit cards regularly for shopping, travel, or bill payments. With smart usage, you can redeem these rewards for a wide range of options like flight tickets, hotel stays, merchandise, gift vouchers, and even cashback.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Earn reward points on every transaction.
  • Redeem points for a variety of rewards including flights, hotels, shopping, etc.
  • Exclusive discounts and deals at partner outlets.
  • Fuel surcharge waiver and utility bill payment offers.
  • Annual fee waiver on reaching spending milestones.

Popular Rewards Credit Cards in India

  • HDFC Regalia Credit Card: Premium rewards and travel benefits.
  • SBI Card ELITE: Welcome e-gift vouchers and milestone rewards.
  • ICICI Coral Credit Card: Cashback, dining, and fuel offers.
  • Axis Bank SELECT Card: Complimentary lounge access and reward points.

Who Should Apply?

  • Frequent shoppers who want to maximize value on spending.
  • Travelers who can benefit from miles or hotel rewards.
  • Individuals looking for lifestyle privileges and premium experiences.
  • People with a good credit score (650+ recommended).

How Do Reward Points Work?

Every time you spend using your rewards credit card, you earn points based on the transaction amount. These points accumulate in your account and can later be redeemed via your bank's reward portal. Some cards offer accelerated rewards on certain categories like travel, dining, fuel, or online shopping. The value of each point varies from bank to bank — typically between ₹0.25 to ₹1 per point.

How to Redeem Reward Points?

  • Login to your bank's net banking or mobile app.
  • Go to the ‘Rewards’ or ‘Credit Card Rewards’ section.
  • Browse the catalogue of redeemable items.
  • Select your preferred reward and complete redemption.

Charges and Fees

  • Annual Fees: Ranges from ₹500 to ₹5000 depending on the card tier.
  • Redemption Fee: Some banks may charge ₹99–₹150 per redemption request.
  • Late Payment Charges: Applicable if minimum payment is missed.

Tips to Maximize Reward Benefits

  • Use your credit card for high-value transactions to earn more points.
  • Keep track of reward point expiry dates.
  • Check for limited-time promotions or bonus point events.
  • Combine points with ongoing offers for better value.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21 to 60 years.
  • Stable monthly income (₹25,000+ preferred).
  • Good credit history and CIBIL score above 650.
  • Proof of address, identity, and income documents.

Documents Required

  • PAN card and Aadhaar card.
  • Recent passport-size photographs.
  • Income proof – Salary slips or bank statements.
  • Address proof – Utility bill, passport, or rental agreement.

How to Apply for a Rewards Credit Card?

  • Visit the official website of the bank or credit card issuer.
  • Choose a suitable rewards card based on your needs.
  • Fill out the application form online with required details.
  • Upload documents or schedule a doorstep KYC pickup.
  • Wait for approval and get the card delivered to your address.

Conclusion

A rewards credit card can be a great tool for saving money and enjoying exclusive benefits, provided it is used wisely. Whether you're a frequent shopper, a traveler, or someone looking for extra value on daily purchases, these cards can give you excellent returns in the form of redeemable rewards. Make sure to review the terms, fees, and reward structure before applying so you can pick the best card for your lifestyle.
