Who Can Apply for a Personal Loan?

  • Age: 21 years to 60 years
  • Income: A steady income (₹25,000 per month or more is generally required)

What Are the Benefits of a Personal Loan?

  • Quick Disbursal: Once approved, the loan amount is transferred directly to your bank account within 24-48 hours.
