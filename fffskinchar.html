Small Ticket Loans (Quick Loans)

Small Ticket Loans, also known as Quick Loans, are short-term, low-value personal loans designed to meet urgent and immediate financial needs. Whether it's paying bills, managing a cash crunch, or handling minor emergencies, Quick Loans offer a hassle-free borrowing experience with minimal documentation and instant disbursal.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Loan amounts starting as low as ₹5,000 and going up to ₹1,00,000.
  • Instant approval and disbursal within minutes of verification.
  • Minimal documentation and fully digital application process.
  • Flexible repayment tenure ranging from 3 to 24 months.
  • Attractive interest rates starting from 1.5% per month.
  • 24x7 availability through online and mobile platforms.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant should be between 21 and 60 years of age.
  • Salaried or self-employed individuals with stable monthly income.
  • Indian resident with valid identity and address proof.
  • Minimum monthly income requirement varies from ₹12,000 to ₹15,000.
  • Good repayment history and CIBIL score preferred (650+).

Loan Usage Examples

  • Medical Expenses: Quick access to funds during medical emergencies.
  • Utility Bills: Avoid late payment penalties by covering shortfalls.
  • Online Shopping: Purchase electronics, gadgets, or essentials.
  • Travel: Book urgent tickets or manage travel costs easily.
  • Education: Pay exam fees or buy study materials instantly.

Fees & Charges

  • Processing Fee: ₹199 to ₹999 depending on loan amount.
  • Interest Rate: 1.5% to 2.5% per month (18% to 30% annually).
  • Late Payment Fee: ₹200 to ₹600 based on EMI amount.
  • Foreclosure Charges: Usually nil or minimal after 3 EMIs.

Digital Process

The entire application process for Quick Loans is digital. Customers can apply via mobile apps or websites, upload documents digitally, and receive funds in their bank account in just a few minutes.

Repayment Flexibility

  • Choose repayment tenure from 3, 6, 12, or 24 months based on affordability.
  • Repayment through auto-debit, UPI, or net banking.
  • Option to foreclose early without extra charges (as per lender policy).

How to Apply

  • Visit the lender’s official website or download their loan app.
  • Register with your mobile number and complete KYC.
  • Fill in employment and income details.
  • Upload PAN, Aadhaar, and bank statements.
  • Receive loan offer, accept terms, and get money disbursed.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card or any valid address proof
  • Salary slips or bank statement (for salaried)
  • ITR or business proofs (for self-employed)
  • Recent Passport-size Photo (if asked)

Conclusion

Small Ticket Loans or Quick Loans offer a convenient solution to your short-term cash needs. With instant disbursal, minimal documentation, and flexible repayment, these loans are ideal for managing everyday financial hiccups. Perfect for salaried individuals, gig workers, and small business owners, Quick Loans make borrowing easy, efficient, and stress-free.
