Axis Bank Personal Loan

Axis Bank Personal Loans provide quick financial assistance for personal needs such as weddings, travel, education, or medical emergencies. With competitive interest rates, minimal documentation, and fast disbursal, Axis Bank makes it simple for salaried and self-employed individuals to manage short-term financial goals with ease.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Loan amount ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹40 lakhs.
  • Flexible repayment tenure from 12 to 60 months.
  • Quick approval and disbursal, often within 48 hours.
  • Minimal documentation required for processing.
  • No collateral or guarantor required.
  • Attractive interest rates starting at 10.49% p.a.*

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age between 21 to 60 years.
  • For salaried employees of public and private companies.
  • Minimum monthly income: ₹15,000 (varies by city).
  • Minimum work experience of 1 year, with 6 months in current job.
  • Good credit history (CIBIL score preferably above 700).

Loan Offers & Usage

  • Weddings: Plan your dream wedding without financial stress.
  • Travel: Finance domestic and international vacations easily.
  • Medical Expenses: Cover emergency or planned healthcare needs.
  • Home Renovation: Upgrade or refurbish your home interiors.
  • Debt Consolidation: Combine multiple EMIs into one manageable loan.

Fees & Charges

  • Processing Fee: Up to 2% of the loan amount + GST.
  • Prepayment Charges: 2% to 5% after 12 EMIs (no prepayment before 12 months).
  • Late Payment Penalty: 2% per month on overdue EMI.
  • Loan Cancellation Fee: Nil, but interest will be charged till cancellation.

Interest Rates

Axis Bank offers competitive interest rates on personal loans starting at 10.49% p.a., depending on the applicant's profile, income, and loan amount. These rates may vary with time and loan eligibility criteria.

How to Apply

  • Visit the Axis Bank website or nearest branch.
  • Navigate to the Personal Loan section and click "Apply Now".
  • Fill in personal, income, and employment details.
  • Upload required KYC and financial documents.
  • Loan is processed and disbursed upon approval.

Documents Required

  • Identity Proof: PAN Card / Aadhaar Card.
  • Address Proof: Passport / Utility Bill / Rent Agreement.
  • Income Proof: Last 3 months’ salary slips or ITR for self-employed.
  • Bank Statement: Last 6 months showing salary credit or income.
  • Photograph: Recent passport-size photo.

Conclusion

Axis Bank Personal Loans offer a reliable and hassle-free way to manage unexpected or planned expenses. With easy documentation, flexible tenures, and competitive interest rates, it’s a practical financial tool for salaried and self-employed individuals alike. Whether you're planning a trip or managing medical costs, Axis Bank provides the support you need, when you need it.
