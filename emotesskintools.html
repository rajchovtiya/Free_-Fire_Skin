Loan Against Property (LAP)

Loan Against Property (LAP) is a secured loan that allows you to leverage your residential or commercial property to meet various financial needs. Whether it’s for business expansion, medical emergencies, or education, LAP offers a high loan amount at attractive interest rates with long repayment tenures.

Key Features & Benefits

  • High loan amounts ranging from ₹5 lakhs to ₹5 crores, depending on property value.
  • Flexible repayment tenure up to 15-20 years.
  • Attractive interest rates starting as low as 8.50% per annum.
  • Loan available against residential, commercial, or rented property.
  • Quick processing and minimal documentation.
  • Funds can be used for business, education, wedding, or medical expenses.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants should be between 21 and 65 years of age.
  • Can be salaried individuals, self-employed professionals, or business owners.
  • Stable income and regular repayment capacity required.
  • Property should be owned and free from legal disputes.
  • Co-applicants allowed to enhance loan eligibility.

Loan Benefits

  • High Value: Get up to 70% of the property's market value as loan.
  • Multi-purpose: Use funds for any legal personal or business purpose.
  • Balance Transfer: Option to transfer existing LAP for lower rates.
  • Tax Benefits: Deduction on interest paid under Section 24(b) of Income Tax Act (if used for business or home renovation).
  • Prepayment Facility: Foreclose your loan anytime with nominal charges.

Fees & Charges

  • Processing Fee: Up to 1% of loan amount + applicable taxes.
  • Prepayment Charges: Usually 2%-4% (may vary with lender type).
  • Late Payment Fee: 2%-3% per month on overdue EMIs.
  • Legal & Valuation Charges: Applicable as per property assessment.

Top Uses of LAP

  • Business expansion or working capital needs.
  • Higher education of children.
  • Medical emergencies and hospitalization expenses.
  • Debt consolidation to lower EMI burden.
  • Wedding or large personal events.

Property Eligibility

  • Residential self-occupied or rented property.
  • Commercial properties like offices or shops.
  • Property must have clear title and legal documentation.
  • Joint ownership allowed (with proper documentation).

How to Apply

  • Visit the bank's website or nearest branch.
  • Click on "Apply Now" for Loan Against Property.
  • Fill out personal, income, and property details.
  • Upload required KYC and property documents.
  • Bank will assess your property and approve the loan if eligible.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card & Aadhaar Card
  • Income Proof (Salary slips, ITR, Form 16, etc.)
  • Property Papers (Title deed, NOC, Approved plan)
  • Bank statements for last 6 months
  • Address and identity proof
  • Passport-size photographs

Conclusion

Loan Against Property is an ideal financial solution for individuals seeking large funds at affordable rates by utilizing their property’s value. With long tenure options, high loan amounts, and multipurpose flexibility, LAP offers financial freedom while keeping your assets intact. Ensure you compare offers and consult with your bank to choose the most suitable plan.
