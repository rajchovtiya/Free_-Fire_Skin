Axis Bank Consumer Durable Loans

Axis Bank Consumer Durable Loans offer a convenient way to finance your everyday lifestyle purchases. Whether you're buying a smartphone, washing machine, LED TV, or kitchen appliances, these loans help you convert your big purchases into easy EMIs with minimal documentation and fast approval—ideal for budget-conscious consumers looking to upgrade without financial stress.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Instant loan approval for eligible customers at partner stores or online.
  • Finance available on a wide range of consumer durables like electronics and appliances.
  • Zero or minimal down payment options available.
  • Flexible repayment tenures ranging from 3 to 24 months.
  • Low or zero interest EMI schemes on select brands and products.
  • Digital application process with quick disbursal.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be at least 21 years old and not more than 60 years at loan maturity.
  • Should be a salaried or self-employed Indian resident with regular income.
  • Must have a valid KYC and income verification documents.
  • Good credit history and a stable financial profile improve chances of approval.

Loan Features

  • Loan Amount: Starting from ₹5,000 up to ₹5 Lakhs (based on product & profile).
  • Repayment Tenure: 3 to 24 months based on product and loan offer.
  • Interest Rate: Starting from 0% on select items with processing fee (T&C apply).
  • EMI Calculator: Use Axis Bank's online EMI calculator to plan repayment.

Top Partner Brands

  • Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Sony, Panasonic, MI, Vivo, Oppo, OnePlus, and more.
  • Available at leading retailers like Croma, Reliance Digital, Vijay Sales, and Amazon/Flipkart.
  • Exclusive seasonal deals and festive EMI offers on branded products.

Zero Cost EMI Offer

Enjoy Zero Cost EMI on select products and brands—pay only the product price divided into equal monthly installments, with no additional interest. Minimal processing fees may apply. Offers available at select partner stores and online platforms.

Processing Fee & Charges

  • Processing Fee: ₹199 to ₹999 depending on product and partner brand.
  • Late Payment Penalty: 2% per month on the overdue EMI amount.
  • Foreclosure Charges: Nil to 5% depending on the scheme selected.

How to Apply

  • Visit any Axis Bank partner store or apply online at Axis Bank website.
  • Select your desired product and choose the Consumer Durable Loan option.
  • Provide necessary details and complete KYC verification.
  • Choose your EMI plan and loan duration.
  • Loan gets approved instantly and product gets delivered or billed immediately.

Documents Required

  • Valid Identity Proof: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, or Passport
  • Address Proof: Utility Bill, Aadhaar, or Rent Agreement
  • Income Proof: Salary slips or bank statements (last 3–6 months)
  • Passport-size photograph (recent)

Conclusion

Axis Bank Consumer Durable Loans are a smart and affordable solution to finance modern lifestyle products. Whether you want to upgrade your phone, home appliances, or gadgets, the easy EMI options make it possible without straining your savings. With instant approvals and tie-ups with top brands, it's an efficient way to enhance your lifestyle with convenience and control.
