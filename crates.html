American Express SmartEarn™ Credit Card

The American Express SmartEarn™ Credit Card is designed for individuals who want to maximize their rewards while enjoying a range of privileges across categories like dining, shopping, travel, and more. With every purchase you make, you earn Membership Rewards® points that can be redeemed for a variety of rewards, including travel, shopping, and entertainment options.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Earn 1 Membership Rewards® point for every ₹50 spent on eligible purchases.
  • Get up to 3x Membership Rewards® points on dining, shopping, and travel expenses.
  • Enjoy a welcome benefit of 2,000 Membership Rewards® points on the first transaction.
  • Access to exclusive discounts and offers with top retail partners, both online and offline.
  • Complimentary access to over 1,200 airport lounges globally through the American Express Global Lounge Collection.
  • Enjoy a host of lifestyle benefits, including access to pre-sale tickets for concerts and events.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The primary cardholder should be between 18 and 65 years of age.
  • Must have a steady income (salaried or self-employed).
  • Indian resident with valid identity and address proof.
  • Minimum monthly income should generally be ₹30,000 or more.
  • Must have a strong credit history (preferably a CIBIL score above 750).

Rewards & Offers

  • Membership Rewards® Points: Earn 1 point for every ₹50 spent. Points can be redeemed for travel, shopping, or statement credit.
  • Dining: Earn up to 3x Membership Rewards® points on dining spends, including restaurants and online food delivery.
  • Shopping: Earn 3x points on shopping with select retail partners, both online and offline.
  • Travel: Get up to 3x Membership Rewards® points on travel-related expenses, including flights, hotels, and car rentals.
  • Entertainment: Enjoy special offers and early access to concert tickets, movie premieres, and more.

Fees & Charges

  • Joining Fee: ₹1,000 (waived with an annual spend of ₹25,000).
  • Annual Fee: ₹3,500 (waived if annual spend exceeds ₹1,00,000).
  • Interest Rate: 3.49% per month (or 41.88% annually).
  • Cash Withdrawal Fee: 2.5% of the withdrawal amount (minimum ₹500).

Welcome Offer

Enjoy 2,000 Membership Rewards® points on your first transaction with the American Express SmartEarn™ Credit Card. This welcome offer makes it easy to start earning rewards right away.

Dining Benefits

Earn up to 3x Membership Rewards® points on dining spends, including restaurants, food delivery services, and bars. You can also enjoy exclusive dining offers at a variety of premium restaurants across the country.

Travel Benefits

  • Earn 3x Membership Rewards® points on travel-related purchases, such as flights, hotels, and car rentals.
  • Access to complimentary airport lounge access worldwide via the American Express Global Lounge Collection.
  • Exclusive travel-related offers and discounts on hotels, tours, and transportation.

How to Apply

  • Visit the American Express website or the nearest branch.
  • Click on "Apply Now" for the SmartEarn™ Credit Card.
  • Fill in the required personal and financial details.
  • Upload necessary KYC documents (ID, address proof, and income proof).
  • Await approval and receive your card within a few working days.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card or Address Proof (Utility Bill, Passport)
  • Income Proof: Salary slips or ITR returns (for self-employed individuals)
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph

Conclusion

The American Express SmartEarn™ Credit Card is an excellent choice for individuals who want to maximize their rewards while enjoying exclusive lifestyle, dining, and travel benefits. With 3x points on dining, shopping, and travel, along with access to premium lounges and entertainment experiences, this card is designed to offer value with every purchase. Whether you're a frequent traveler or someone who enjoys fine dining and shopping, the SmartEarn™ Credit Card is a rewarding companion for your everyday spending.
