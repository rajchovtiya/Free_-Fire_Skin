IndianOil Axis Bank Credit Card Overview

The IndianOil Axis Bank Credit Card is tailored for individuals seeking substantial savings on fuel expenditures. With attractive reward structures and additional perks, it stands out as a valuable financial tool for daily commuters and online shoppers alike.

:contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Key Features & Benefits

  • Welcome Benefit: Earn up to ₹250 cashback on fuel transactions within the first 30 days of card issuance.
  • Fuel Rewards: Receive 20 EDGE Reward Points for every ₹100 spent at IndianOil outlets, translating to a 4% value back.
  • Online Shopping Rewards: Gain 5 EDGE Reward Points per ₹100 spent on online shopping, equating to a 1% value back.
  • Fuel Surcharge Waiver: Enjoy a 1% waiver on fuel surcharge for transactions between ₹400 and ₹4,000, capped at ₹50 per month.
  • Annual Fee Waiver: Spend ₹3.5 lakh in a year to waive the annual fee for the subsequent year.
  • UPI Integration: Seamlessly link your credit card with UPI for convenient payments.
:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Fees & Charges

  • Joining Fee: ₹500 + GST.
  • Annual Fee: ₹500 + GST (waived on annual spends of ₹3.5 lakh).
  • Interest Rate: 3.6% per month (52.86% annually).
  • Cash Withdrawal Fee: 2.5% of the amount withdrawn or ₹500, whichever is higher.
  • Foreign Currency Markup: 3.5% of the transaction amount.
:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: Applicant should be between 18 and 70 years.
  • Residency: Must be a resident of India.
  • Income: Stable income source; specific income criteria may apply.
  • Credit Score: A good credit history is preferred.
:contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

Required Documents

  • Proof of Identity: PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Passport, etc.
  • Proof of Address: Utility bills, rental agreement, etc.
  • Income Proof: Salary slips, bank statements, ITR, etc.
  • Photographs: Recent passport-sized photographs.
:contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

How to Apply

Interested applicants can apply for the IndianOil Axis Bank Credit Card through the official Axis Bank website or by visiting the nearest Axis Bank branch. Ensure all required documents are ready for a smooth application process.

:contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

Additional Benefits

  • EDGE Rewards: Redeem accumulated points for a variety of options including vouchers, merchandise, and more.
  • EMI Conversion: Convert high-value purchases into easy EMIs.
  • Secure Transactions: Enhanced security features for safe and secure transactions.
:contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

Contact Information

For more details or assistance, contact Axis Bank customer care at 1860-419-5555 or visit the official website at www.axisbank.com.
