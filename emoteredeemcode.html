Axis Bank Gold Loan

Axis Bank Gold Loan offers a quick and secure way to meet your financial needs by leveraging the value of your gold ornaments. Whether it’s for business, education, medical emergencies, or personal requirements, this loan provides instant liquidity with minimal documentation and attractive interest rates.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Loan amount ranging from ₹25,001 to ₹25 lakhs based on gold valuation.
  • Attractive interest rates starting as low as 10.49% p.a.
  • Flexible repayment tenures from 6 months to 36 months.
  • Quick disbursal within hours of document submission.
  • Secure locker facility for storing pledged gold.
  • No prepayment or foreclosure charges on floating rate loans.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant should be 18 years or older.
  • Available to salaried, self-employed, and agriculturists.
  • Should possess gold jewelry of 18 to 22 carats purity.
  • Loan available for Indian residents only.
  • No income proof required for loans up to a specific amount.

Loan Types

  • Term Loan: Repay principal and interest as per agreed schedule.
  • Overdraft Facility: Withdraw funds as needed within the sanctioned limit.
  • Bullet Repayment: Pay both interest and principal at the end of the loan tenure.

Interest Rates & Charges

  • Interest Rate: Starts from 10.49% per annum.
  • Processing Fee: Up to 1% of the loan amount (minimum ₹500).
  • Prepayment Charges: Nil for floating rate loans.
  • Penal Charges: 2% per month on overdue amounts.

Loan Disbursement Process

The gold loan is disbursed quickly after evaluating the gold’s purity and weight. The pledged gold is securely stored, and the approved loan amount is credited to your bank account within hours.

Repayment Options

  • EMI-based repayment for term loans.
  • Flexible withdrawal and interest payment in case of overdraft.
  • Bullet repayment option for short-term liquidity needs.

Security & Safety

Axis Bank ensures complete safety of your gold through high-security vaults and insurance coverage. You receive a loan receipt with the weight and purity details of your pledged ornaments.

How to Apply

  • Visit the nearest Axis Bank branch with your gold ornaments.
  • Fill in the loan application form.
  • Submit KYC documents.
  • Gold will be evaluated and loan eligibility will be calculated.
  • Loan disbursed to your account upon approval.

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity: PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Passport
  • Proof of Address: Utility Bill, Aadhaar, Driving License
  • Passport-size Photograph

Conclusion

Axis Bank Gold Loan is an ideal solution for instant financial needs without selling your gold. With flexible tenures, competitive interest rates, and hassle-free processing, it provides a secure and smart borrowing option backed by your valuable assets.
