Axis Bank MY Zone Credit Card

The Axis Bank MY Zone Credit Card is tailored for young shoppers and lifestyle enthusiasts. It offers exciting discounts on fashion, dining, entertainment, and more—making it a go-to card for millennials and trendsetters. With its affordable annual fee and valuable reward structure, the MY Zone Card packs style and savings together.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Get complimentary SonyLiv Premium annual subscription on first spend.
  • Flat 40% off on partner restaurants via EazyDiner.
  • Buy One Get One Free offer on movie tickets via Paytm Movies (up to ₹200 per month).
  • 1% fuel surcharge waiver at all fuel stations across India.
  • 4 EDGE Reward Points for every ₹200 spent.
  • Spend ₹5,000 within 45 days of card issuance to get welcome benefits.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Primary cardholder should be between 18 to 70 years of age.
  • Should be either a salaried or self-employed individual with a stable income.
  • Resident of India with a valid Indian address and identity proof.
  • Minimum monthly income should generally be ₹15,000 or more.
  • Should have a decent credit history and CIBIL score (preferably above 700).

Rewards & Offers

  • EDGE Rewards: Earn 4 EDGE Reward Points for every ₹200 spent.
  • Dining: Get up to 40% off on dining bills with EazyDiner Prime access.
  • Entertainment: Monthly BOGO offer on movie tickets (up to ₹200/month) on Paytm Movies.
  • Shopping: Enjoy lifestyle and fashion discounts with partner merchants during festive seasons.
  • Fuel: 1% surcharge waiver at fuel stations (max waiver ₹400/month).

Fees & Charges

  • Joining Fee: ₹500 (waived on spending ₹5,000 in 45 days).
  • Annual Fee: ₹500 from second year onwards (can be waived on annual spend of ₹35,000).
  • Interest Rate: 3.6% per month (or 52.86% annually).
  • Cash Withdrawal Fee: 2.5% of the amount (min ₹500).

SonyLiv Subscription Offer

Enjoy SonyLiv Premium annual subscription as a welcome gift by using your card for any transaction within 30 days of issuance. This offer includes access to exclusive shows, live sports, and more entertainment content.

Movie Benefits

  • Enjoy “Buy One Get One Free” on movie tickets every month via Paytm Movies.
  • Maximum discount per month is ₹200.
  • Use coupon code shared on Axis Bank app or Paytm offer section.

Dining Discounts

Axis MY Zone Credit Card holders can enjoy instant discounts up to 40% at partner restaurants through EazyDiner Prime. Just book your table via the EazyDiner app and pay using your MY Zone Credit Card.

Fuel Surcharge Waiver

  • Waiver on 1% fuel surcharge on transactions between ₹400 to ₹4,000.
  • Valid across all fuel stations in India.
  • Maximum benefit: ₹400 per month.

How to Apply

  • Visit the Axis Bank website or nearest branch.
  • Click on "Apply Now" for MY Zone Credit Card.
  • Fill in the required personal and income details.
  • Upload KYC documents (ID, Address, Income proof).
  • Await quick verification and card dispatch upon approval.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card or Address Proof (Utility Bill, Passport)
  • Income Proof: Salary slips or ITR returns (for self-employed)
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph

Conclusion

The Axis Bank MY Zone Credit Card is a lifestyle-centric card with features specially designed for urban youth. From entertainment benefits to dining privileges and stylish rewards, this card makes your everyday spending more rewarding. With a low fee and high-value offers, it’s one of the most appealing entry-level lifestyle cards in India.
