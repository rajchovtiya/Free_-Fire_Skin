Axis Bank Home Loans

Axis Bank Home Loans are designed to help you purchase, build, or renovate your dream home. With attractive interest rates, flexible tenures, and a hassle-free application process, Axis Bank offers tailor-made solutions for all your housing needs.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Loan amounts starting from ₹3 lakh and up to ₹5 crore based on eligibility.
  • Flexible repayment tenure ranging up to 30 years.
  • Attractive floating and fixed interest rate options.
  • Quick loan processing with minimal documentation.
  • Top-up loan and balance transfer facility available.
  • Doorstep service for documentation and verification.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants should be aged between 21 to 65 years.
  • Can be salaried, self-employed, or professionals with a stable income.
  • Resident of India with valid ID and address proof.
  • Good credit history and CIBIL score (typically above 700).
  • Minimum income criteria as per Axis Bank’s internal policies.

Rewards & Offers

  • Balance Transfer Offer: Transfer your existing home loan at lower interest rates.
  • Top-Up Loan: Avail additional loan amount on top of your existing home loan.
  • PMAY Benefits: Eligible borrowers can apply under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY).
  • Tax Benefits: Get tax deductions on both principal and interest payments.
  • Zero Prepayment Penalty: For floating rate home loans.

Fees & Charges

  • Processing Fee: Up to 1% of loan amount + applicable taxes.
  • Prepayment Charges: NIL for floating rate loans.
  • Late Payment Charges: 2% per month on overdue EMI.
  • Legal & Technical Charges: As per actuals (case-by-case).

Interest Rate Details

Interest rates for Axis Bank Home Loans start from 8.70% p.a. and vary based on your credit score, loan amount, and profile. Both floating and fixed rate options are available, and rates may be revised periodically by the bank.

Top-Up Loan Facility

  • Available for existing Axis Bank home loan customers with good repayment history.
  • Can be used for renovation, furnishing, or personal use.
  • Minimal paperwork and quick approval process.

Balance Transfer

Switch your existing home loan to Axis Bank at a lower interest rate and save on EMIs. Enjoy additional benefits like top-up loan and faster service through a dedicated team.

Tax Benefits

  • Claim deduction up to ₹1.5 lakh on principal under Section 80C.
  • Claim deduction up to ₹2 lakh on interest under Section 24(b).
  • First-time home buyers may claim additional ₹50,000 under Section 80EE (subject to eligibility).

How to Apply

  • Visit Axis Bank’s official website or nearest branch.
  • Choose “Home Loan” and fill in the online application form.
  • Upload necessary KYC, income, and property documents.
  • Bank will process, verify, and sanction the loan.
  • Loan amount is disbursed after final approval and agreement signing.

Documents Required

  • Identity Proof (PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Passport)
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Aadhaar, Passport)
  • Income Proof (Salary Slips, Bank Statements, ITRs)
  • Property Documents (Sale deed, agreement, NOC, title documents)
  • Passport-size Photograph

Conclusion

Axis Bank Home Loans make owning a home simpler and more affordable. With customized solutions, transparent charges, and expert assistance, Axis Bank ensures a smooth and rewarding home loan experience for every customer.
