American Express Membership Rewards Credit Card

The American Express Membership Rewards Credit Card is designed for those who value premium lifestyle, exclusive rewards, and exceptional service. Whether you're shopping, dining, or traveling, this card offers a world of privileges tailored to your needs. Enjoy superior rewards on every purchase and unlock exclusive experiences with your Membership Rewards points.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Earn 1 Membership Rewards Point for every ₹50 spent on eligible purchases.
  • Welcome benefit of 2,500 Membership Rewards Points on card approval.
  • Up to 3x Membership Rewards Points on select categories such as dining, travel, and online shopping.
  • Exclusive discounts and offers with premium brands like Amazon, Flipkart, and more.
  • Travel-related benefits including complimentary access to over 1,200 airport lounges worldwide.
  • Access to VIP events, concerts, and experiences through the American Express Platinum Service.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Primary cardholder should be between 18 to 65 years of age.
  • Must have a stable income (salaried or self-employed).
  • Indian resident with valid address and identity proof.
  • Minimum monthly income should generally be ₹50,000 or more.
  • Should have a strong credit history and CIBIL score (preferably above 750).

Rewards & Offers

  • Membership Rewards Points: Earn 1 point for every ₹50 spent. Points can be redeemed for travel, shopping, and more.
  • Dining: Up to 20% off at select restaurants globally with the American Express Global Dining Collection.
  • Travel: 3x Membership Rewards Points on travel bookings through American Express Travel or selected partners.
  • Shopping: Enjoy exclusive shopping benefits with premium retail partners.
  • Entertainment: Access to exclusive events and experiences like movie premieres and VIP concerts.

Fees & Charges

  • Joining Fee: ₹1,000 (waived with annual spending of ₹50,000).
  • Annual Fee: ₹5,000 (can be waived if you meet the annual spend requirement).
  • Interest Rate: 3.49% per month (or 41.88% annually).
  • Cash Withdrawal Fee: 3% of the amount withdrawn (min ₹300).

Welcome Offer

Receive 2,500 Membership Rewards Points as a welcome gift when you spend ₹10,000 within the first 60 days of card issuance. This is just the beginning of the rewards you can enjoy with the American Express Membership Rewards Credit Card.

Dining Benefits

American Express cardholders enjoy special privileges like up to 20% off at partner restaurants around the world. Simply present your American Express card at the time of payment to enjoy these exclusive dining discounts.

Airport Lounge Access

  • Complimentary access to over 1,200 airport lounges worldwide through the American Express Global Lounge Collection.
  • Unlimited access for the cardholder and a guest on international and domestic flights.

How to Apply

  • Visit the American Express website or nearest branch.
  • Click on "Apply Now" for the Membership Rewards Credit Card.
  • Fill in the required personal and income details.
  • Upload KYC documents (ID, Address, Income proof).
  • Upon approval, enjoy instant access to your card and benefits.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card or Address Proof (Utility Bill, Passport)
  • Income Proof: Salary slips or ITR returns (for self-employed)
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph

Conclusion

The American Express Membership Rewards Credit Card is a perfect companion for those who seek premium rewards, exclusive offers, and superior lifestyle benefits. From travel perks to entertainment access, this card elevates your everyday purchases into extraordinary experiences. Its high rewards rate and exclusive privileges make it an excellent choice for anyone who values luxury and convenience in their financial tools.
