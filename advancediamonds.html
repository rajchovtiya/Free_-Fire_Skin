Who Can Apply for a Personal Loan?

  • Age: 21 years to 60 years
  • Income: A steady income (₹25,000 per month or more is generally required)
  • Employment: Salaried, self-employed, or business owners
  • CIBIL Score: A score of 650 or above is ideal, but some lenders may approve loans with lower scores.

What Are the Benefits of a Personal Loan?

  • No Collateral Required: You don’t need to give any property or valuables as security.

  • Flexibility: Use the loan for anything – medical bills, wedding expenses, home renovations, travel, etc.

  • Easy Repayments: Choose a loan tenure that works for you, with easy monthly installments.

  • Quick Disbursal: Once approved, the loan amount is transferred directly to your bank account within 24-48 hours.
