Flipkart Axis Bank Credit Card – Overview

The Flipkart Axis Bank Credit Card is one of the most rewarding co-branded credit cards in India, designed for online shoppers who frequently use Flipkart and its partner platforms. With generous cashback offers, exclusive discounts, and a low annual fee, it is a value-packed option for budget-conscious yet savvy users. Whether you're buying electronics, groceries, fashion, or booking your next trip, this card offers a great way to save while spending smartly.

Key Features & Benefits

  • 5% unlimited cashback on Flipkart and Myntra
  • 4% cashback on preferred merchants like Swiggy, Uber, PVR, Cleartrip, and others
  • 1.5% cashback on all other eligible transactions
  • Welcome benefit of ₹500 Flipkart voucher on card activation (within 30 days of issuance)
  • Fuel surcharge waiver of 1% at all fuel stations across India (max ₹500 per month)
  • Low annual fee of ₹500 + taxes (waived from 2nd year onwards on ₹2 lakh annual spend)
  • Contactless payments supported for fast and secure transactions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 18 years for primary cardholders
  • Indian resident with a valid address and identity proof
  • Stable source of income (salaried or self-employed)
  • Good credit history; a CIBIL score of 700+ is ideal
  • No prior record of payment defaults with Axis Bank or other lenders

Fee & Charges

  • Joining fee: ₹500 + applicable taxes
  • Annual fee: ₹500 + taxes (waived from 2nd year if spends exceed ₹2 lakhs)
  • Interest rate (APR): 3.6% per month (approx. 52.86% annually)
  • Late payment fee: ₹0 to ₹1200 depending on statement amount
  • Over-limit fee: 2.5% of over-limit amount (min ₹500)
  • Foreign currency markup fee: 3.5% on international transactions

Reward Program Details

  • Cashback is auto-credited to your statement every month
  • No minimum redemption threshold – cashback is credited even for ₹1
  • There’s no cap on total cashback, ensuring better long-term savings
  • Most utility bill payments and wallet reloads may not earn cashback

Advantages of Flipkart Axis Bank Credit Card

  • Best for Flipkart Lovers: If you shop frequently on Flipkart or Myntra, this card offers unmatched value with 5% cashback.
  • Multiple Cashback Opportunities: Enjoy rewards not just on Flipkart, but also on Swiggy, PVR, Uber, and more.
  • Affordable Fees: The ₹500 fee is minimal compared to the benefits, and it can be waived easily.
  • Simple and Automatic Cashback: No need to redeem manually or remember points – just earn and save!
  • Fuel Savings: Waiver on fuel surcharge reduces your overall travel costs too.

How to Apply

  • Visit the official Axis Bank website or Flipkart’s co-branded credit card section.
  • Fill in your personal, professional, and income details accurately.
  • Upload necessary KYC documents like PAN, Aadhaar, and address proof.
  • Once verified, Axis Bank will approve and dispatch your card within 7-10 working days.
  • Track your application online through Axis Bank’s portal or customer care.

Comparison with Other Shopping Credit Cards

  • Amazon Pay ICICI Credit Card: 5% cashback on Amazon (for Prime members), lifetime free card.
  • HDFC Millennia: Up to 5% cashback on top brands but with monthly capping and minimum transaction conditions.
  • SBI SimplyCLICK: 10X rewards on online shopping, but redemption is in points, not direct cashback.
  • Verdict: Flipkart Axis Bank Card stands out for its direct cashback model and ease of use.

Customer Support

Axis Bank offers 24x7 customer care support via phone, email, and branch visits. You can also manage your card using the Axis Bank mobile app or net banking platform. For issues related to rewards, blocking cards, billing disputes, or increasing limits, contact Axis Bank’s toll-free helpline at 1860-419-5555.

Final Verdict

If you are a frequent shopper on Flipkart or its affiliated platforms, the Flipkart Axis Bank Credit Card offers unbeatable value with its 5% cashback, welcome bonus, and low maintenance cost. It’s ideal for students, young professionals, and budget-conscious families who prefer online shopping. With a clean reward system, practical benefits, and Axis Bank’s trusted service, this card is one of the top choices in the co-branded card space today.
